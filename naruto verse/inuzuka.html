The Inuzuka clan are a clan of ninja in the leaf village who use ninja hounds. The clan members are born with dog features such as canine teeth and extremely sensitive sense of smell. They usually have red triangle paint on the right and left side of their cheek. They have a practice of given members dogs as life time companions.
