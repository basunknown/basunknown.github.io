The hidden waterfall is a village located in an unnamed, small country and is lead by the First Takikage, Cale Neito. The village was first founded by Samuel Neito and further built upon by his son, Cale Neito who lead the village into prosperity by becoming a Shinobi. It is rumoured that the Waterfall Village is the host of many secrets and supernatural things. This is what drew in small groups of people from surrounding settlements which moulded the village into what it is today.
