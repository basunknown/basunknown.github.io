Uchiha clan was one of the most influencial and powerful clans of the hidden leaf village. They have been at war with the Senju clan way before the village was built. They were one of the clans that founded the hidden leaf village. Before the hidden leaf village village was founded, children belonging to the clan were trained very early to be shinobis and sent too war even though they were still kids. The Uchiha clan have produced very powerful shinobi's for the hidden leaf village.
