The Land of Wind is a vast land consisting of mostly desert, due to little rain fall. The Land of wind shares border with the land of fire. The head of the land of wind is the feudal lord of the land. The land of wind is also considered part of the 5 great nations because of it vast land and shinobi village.

The hidden sand is a village within the land of wind and home to the nation's shinobi. The head of the hidden sand village is called the Kazekage. The villages first kazekage was Reto and the village is currently at it 5th kazekage, Gaara. Within the years the hidden sand and the hidden leaf village have had great partnership.
