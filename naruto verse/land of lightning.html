The land of Lightning is one of the five great nations. It country consists of tall mountains and the frequent thunder in the montain areas seems to be the reason for it name. The head of the Nation is the feudal lord and it shinobi village is the hidden cloud village.

The hidden cloud village is home to the shinobi of the land of lightning and it head is called the Raikage. The village was founded by the 1st Raikage and it is litterally hidden in the clouds, since it is located at the top of mountains. The hidden cloud have been stealing jutsus from various villages, before the time of peace and there was an incident were they tried to steal the hyuga clans byakugan. Since then the hidden cloud joined into the war against the akatsuki with all the four great villages, their shared pain brought them together after the war, leading the hidden cloud to peace and ending the major conflicts with the other villages.
